Airfryer Chicken

Fried chicken with no oil, your welcome.

Fried chicken in and airfryer. The easiest way to eat healthy and delicious.

    Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Seasoning
  • Airfryer
  • Flour
  • Egg
      • Steps:
  • Take your chicken and add the seasoning to it and let it rest for at least 30 minutes
  • Give it and egg wash and then cover it in a mixture of flour and the same seasoning you put on the chicken
  • Put the chicken inside the airfryer and put the airfryer to 390 degrees fahrenheit for 25 minutes
  • Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes and enjoy!
