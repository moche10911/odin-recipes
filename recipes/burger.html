Burger

    Ingredients:
  • Burger Bread
  • 120gr ground meat 15% fat
  • Favorite cheese
  • Caramelized onions
  • Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Preferred sauce
      • Steps:
  • Get your burger patty ready with the form put salt and pepper to them
  • Get a pan with oil ripping hot
  • Once your pan is hot put your patty and flip it in 3 minutes
  • While your patty is cooking, toast your buns
  • Once you flip your burger put 2 slices of cheese on top so it melts
  • Arm your burger as you prefer and enjoy!
